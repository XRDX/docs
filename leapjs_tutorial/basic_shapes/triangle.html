三角形 triangle¶

绘制方法¶

triangle(x1, y1, x2, y2, x3, y3, *color)

代码举例：

canvas.showAxis();
triangle(50, 50, 100, 50, 100, 100);
triangle(150, 100, 170, 50, 200, 100, 'red');

fill(false);
triangle(50, 150, 100, 150, 100, 200);
triangle(150, 200, 170, 150, 200, 200, 'red');
